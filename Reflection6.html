Assignment - Web Development

Reflection

As we progressed me and my team realised that are websites must be unique, so some team members went one way down the dark and grey colours whereas I stayed using blues from materiel design. Although this may make my website stand out from others, I think it is a good change and I shall be developing it more in the future shall I build another website. Overall, I think working in a team has been good because we can explain and discuss ideas together and work out what can be done and what cannot be done in the time scale given. We can also work together to test each other’s code and work out what is wrong and the problems with it.

Working in a team makes you susceptible to change and will definitely make a better website. I would choose to work in a team next time rather than on my own for the reasons above. It helps with testing, you can share ideas and it makes the process more fun in the long run. There may be some advantages of working on your own like not spending so much time on another person’s web page and only on your own one.
