My Specialism

Cyber Security is easily on of the biggest issues 21st century technology is facing. Weather it is having bank details stolen or foreign attacks on British computers. This course specifically focuses on teaching you the skills that you need to try and tackle these issues. Networking issues and making sure Websites are focused on in this course

I will specialise in my second and third years on programming specifics to web applications and develop expertise so that I can design, programme and optimise graphics for 2D and 3D platforms.

In the 2nd year you have an opportunity to do a group project and 3rd year you will do an individual dissertation.
